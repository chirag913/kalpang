Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What exactly is Nabhi Therapy and is it safe?
+
Nabhi Therapy is an ancient Ayurvedic practice of applying therapeutic herbal oils to the navel. It is completely non-invasive, deeply nourishing, and safe for all ages. The navel connects to over 72,000 nerve endings in the body, making it one of the most powerful points for healing.
How long before I see results?
+
Many clients report feeling a difference within 7–10 days of consistent practice. Deeper healing typically unfolds over 21–90 days depending on the condition. Our programs are designed with this timeline in mind — we believe in sustainable, lasting transformation rather than quick fixes.
Can I join online if I'm not in Noida?
+
Absolutely. All our yoga classes, workshops, and consultation sessions are available online via Zoom. We ship our products across India. Many of our most transformed clients are from across the country and even abroad.
Are your products chemical-free and genuinely natural?
+
Yes, completely. Every product is made with cold-pressed, traditionally processed herbs and oils — no artificial fragrances, no preservatives, no chemicals. We source our herbs directly from Himalayan farmers and process them in small batches to ensure purity and potency.
I am on medications — can I still join?
+
Yes, our healing practices are complementary and do not interfere with medications. We work alongside your existing treatment. Over time, many participants find they need less medication — but any changes should always be made under medical supervision. We guide you safely through this transition.
